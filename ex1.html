Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It’s creamy, delicious, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 90 g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. In a bowl, beat eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix with pancetta in the pan.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
